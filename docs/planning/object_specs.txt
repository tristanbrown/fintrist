### Stream ###
- Hosts a series of studies
- Stores a refresh interval.
- Triggers each study to run, in order, on the refresh interval. 
- Centralizes alerts?
- Unique to each user.

### Study ###
- An object that associates a specific process with specific inputs. 
- Stores the output data. 
- Inputs can be other specific studies.
- Unique to each user. 
- Can be associated with more than one Stream.
- Multiple copies of the same Study in the same Stream are nonsensical. 

### Process ###
- A function that generates data. 
- May or may not take data inputs. 
- Universally available.
